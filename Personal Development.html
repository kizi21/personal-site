STRENGTHS

  • Emotional Intelligence

    • According to Segal,Smith,Robinson and Shubin emotional intelligence is defined as the ability
    to understand,use and manage your own emotions in positive ways to relieve stress,communicate
    effectively,empathize with others and overcome challenges and defuse conflict. Personally, for
    me it allows my emotions not to get the best of me as such I consider this my biggest strength.
    This because it allows things not to affect me deeply as well as helps me not to get overwhelmed
    when I find myself in emotional situations.

  • Ability to handle stressful situations

    • In January my grandmother had an accident in which she had to be admitted to the hospital. I remained
    very calm in this situation and as such could handle calling the ambulance,and preparing everything for
    her to be admitted. The ability to handle stressful situations allows me to not feel overwhelmed or freak out
    if something bad happens.

  • My open-mindedness

    • I consider myself to be an open-minded person. This has become a strength of mine because it allows me to
    think outside of the box and understand other persons thought process. It provides as a good advantage in the
    field of I.T. as it allows me to handle programming questions from different angles as well as figure out errors.

Reflecting on these strengths I do not believe I have really been trying to actively develop myself in these past three years.
In terms of my emotional intelligence I feel like I've developed the nasty habit of ignoring my emotions instead of truly processing
them. Yes, I can analayze and know exactly what others and myself are feeling. However,for my feelings I tend to ignore them when I cant
bother to process what I am feelings. In terms of my ability to handle stressful situations I believe that is not something I need to
develop any more.
